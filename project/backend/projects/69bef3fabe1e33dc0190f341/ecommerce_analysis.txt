Ecommerce Website Analysis
==========================
Introduction:
An ecommerce website is an online platform that allows customers to browse and purchase products or services over the internet. The website typically includes features such as product catalogs, shopping carts, payment gateways, and order management systems.

Features of an Ecommerce Website:
1. User Registration and Login
2. Product Browsing and Search
3. Shopping Cart and Checkout
4. Payment Gateway Integration
5. Order Management System
6. Inventory Management System
7. Shipping and Delivery Integration
8. Customer Reviews and Ratings
9. Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
10. Analytics and Reporting
